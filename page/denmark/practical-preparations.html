Practical preparations

Before arriving in Denmark, there's plenty of practicalities that are useful to prepare, learn about and find more information about. I'll discuss them in this post.

Travel

You can plan your trip from the airport to your housing in advance using the website Rejseplan, there's an English language option available. It's possible to buy single way tickets, but if you plan on using public transport a couple of times more, buying a transport card (Rejsekort) is recommended.

Not using a bike in Denmark would be stupid, so you'll want to buy one as soon as possible. Some sites: guloggratis.dk (Danish equivalent of Craigslist), Secondhandbike.dk (English site).

Communication

A prepaid plan is the best option, because you can only get a contract if you have already registered in Denmark. I recommend Oister Go!, they have plenty of data for a reasonable price, you can buy a SIM card at post offices and phone stores. If you need to call abroad, it's better to use Skype.
